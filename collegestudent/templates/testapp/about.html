IGNTU : Regional Campus Manipur

About the Regional Campus, Manipur -
The Indira Gandhi National Tribal University, Regional Campus Manipur (IGNTU-RCM) was established in 2009 as a Regional Centre of IGNTU, Amarkantak. The IGNTU-RCM is presently functioning from a temporary campus situated at Adimjati Shiksha Ashram, Imphal. The permanent campus of IGNTU-RCM is located in the green and picturesque village of Makhan (Senapati District) which is 25 kms from Imphal. The construction of the campus is presently being undertaken in the 301 acres large area of land allocated by the Government of Manipur. The campus presently has four departments viz. Departments of Political Science, Sociology, Social Work, and Tribal Studies offering Master Degree Programmes. The campus has also started offering Ph.D course in Political Science and Social Work from academic session 2014-15.


How to Reach IGNTU-RCM ?
The nearest Airport is Tulihal International Airport, Imphal which is approximately 31 kms from the campus in Makhan. Imphal airport is connected by daily direct flights from Delhi, Kolkata and Guwahati. The journey from airport to the campus can be completed by road through taxi and auto services available at the airport.

The Indira Gandhi National Tribal University Regional Campus-Manipur (IGNTU-RCM) came into existence on 9th September, 2009. The campus with an area of 301 acres is located at Makhan, P O Awang Sekmai, under Kangpokpi District, Manipur. The university is known for its scenic beauty amid those colourful people. This university is the lone Regional Campus of Indira Gandhi National Tribal University Amarkantak (M.P). University is the highest centre for learning and obtaining knowledge of all kinds. Intending to achieve its goal, the Regional Campus Manipur is providing PG and Ph.D programmes in five disciplines- namely, Political Science & Human Rights, Social Work, Computer Science, Sociology, Tribal Studies and Physical Education. The primary objectives of my university as per its ordinance are to provide avenues of higher education and research facilities primarily for the tribal population of India. Further, to disseminate and advance knowledge by providing instructional and research facilities in tribal art, culture, custom, tradition, economy and ways of life. It also focuses on formulating tribal centric development model by organizing various academic activities such as conferences, workshops, seminars, research works etc. on issues relating to tribal nature. However, the Region Campus Manipur stands open without cultural and territorial limits. The campus is committed to overall growth in academic, intellectual, and professional and also to a cordial relationship among the students, faculty and staff by providing a peaceful academic atmosphere and congenial environment. The Regional Campus Manipur is known to the outside world for its wide-ranging knowledge, determination as well and devotion of the faculty, and staff and commendable achievements of the students. The university will continue to strive for social transformation, development of the nation and service to mankind as a part of good citizens.
